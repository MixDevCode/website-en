Sobre mí

This is me with one of the mascots of the house

Hello everyone! I'm Lucas Scifo, a programming enthusiast with over a decade of experience in this exciting world. I was born and raised in Monte Caseros, Corrientes, Argentina, where I immersed myself in the fascinating universe of computer science from an early age.

My education includes obtaining a degree as a Computer Technician, and more recently, I graduated as a Superior Technician in Software Development. Throughout my career, I have acquired knowledge through various courses, excelling in areas such as PC Maintenance and Repair, Fullstack PHP, and Audio and Video Repair (Electronics). Although my background is broad, my focus is primarily on programming, where I find my true passion and strength.

My work approach is characterized by efficiency and effective problem-solving. I specialize in challenging projects and enjoy the creativity that arises when facing new challenges. My ability to work under pressure and adapt to dynamic environments are aspects I have perfected throughout my career.

In addition to my commitment to programming, I have a particular fondness for simple and quick games, reflecting my desire to find ingenious solutions promptly. I am excited about the opportunities that the programming work offers, and I am ready to contribute my experience and grow professionally in this exciting field.

If you are interested in learning more about my work and projects, I invite you to explore my website. Welcome to my digital space!
